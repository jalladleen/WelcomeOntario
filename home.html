Canada's Latest Newcomer Essentials - February 1st 2021

Every first day of the month throughout year, there are endless categories that we have to be fully knowledgeable before stepping foot on Canada. However, there is too much information and over times can be quite overwhelming to balance every little thing that ranges from taxes, child benefit, housing plans, keeping an eye on the amount of limitations and so on. Therefore, we have compressed essential information into three categories that is updated frequently to ease that stressful process for newcomers, immigrants, and people that want to learn more about Canada.

Apply for PR

Obtaining permanent residency enables you to begin your life as a non-citizen in Canada with no considerable limitations regarding work-status and time staying in Canada. Although you are not a citizen (yet), you are considered to have similar responsibilities and rights to a Canadian citizen. Thus, applying for PR is incredibly essential.

Guide to the CCRF

Adopted in 1982, Canadian Charter of Rights and Freedoms are bill of rights that guarantees to every Canadian citizens. Such rights include freedom of religion, democratic rights, equality of all races, official language rights and so on. It is an interesting time to glance over the Constitution of Canadian land.
